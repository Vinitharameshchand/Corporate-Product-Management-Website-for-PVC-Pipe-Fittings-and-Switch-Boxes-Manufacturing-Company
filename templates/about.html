About Vinitha PVC

Pioneering Excellence in PVC Manufacturing Since 2005

Our Legacy & Mission

Founded in 2005, Vinitha PVC Manufacturing Company has grown from a small local workshop to one of India's leading manufacturers of PVC pipe fittings and electrical switch boxes.

Our mission is to provide the highest quality manufacturing solutions that empower builders, electricians, and plumbers to create safer and more durable infrastructures.
